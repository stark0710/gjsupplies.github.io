About GJ Supplies

Welcome to GJ Supplies, your number one source for all things Home & Daily Use. We're dedicated to giving you the best of household essentials, with a focus on dependability, customer service, and uniqueness.

Founded in 2024, GJ Supplies has come a long way from its beginnings. When we first started out, our passion for helping people be more eco-friendly and organized drove us to do intense research, and gave us the impetus to turn hard work and inspiration into to a booming online store. We now serve customers all over India, and are thrilled to be a part of the quirky, eco-friendly, fair trade wing of the home goods industry.

We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
