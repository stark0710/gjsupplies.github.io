About GJ Supplies

GJ Supplies was founded with a simple mission: To make electronics and robotics accessible to every student and hobbyist in India.

We understand the frustration of finding genuine components at reasonable prices. Whether you are building your first line-follower robot, working on a final year engineering project, or prototyping an IoT device, we have the parts you need.

Why Choose Us?

  • Quality Assured: Every component is tested before shipping.
  • Student Friendly: Affordable prices and project guidance.
  • Fast Delivery: We ship across India with reliable courier partners.
  • Technical Support: Stuck on a project? Our team is here to help.
